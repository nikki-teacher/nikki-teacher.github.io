Meet Nikki

Nikki is the passionate founder and driving force behind Centrepoint.

With over 15 years of consecutive teaching experience, Nikki's journey in education has been marked by dedication, innovation, and a commitment to empowering learners of all ages.

Professional Background
Nikki graduated from the University of Aberdeen in 2009 with a Master of Arts Honours Degree in History.
She further specialised in Primary Education at Postgraduate Level in 2010.
Nikki has extensive experience working within the Curriculum for Excellence and the English National Curriculum across various primary levels.
She has managed and supported teams of teachers nationwide, fostering holistic education environments that inspire and make a real difference.
Continuous Learning and Development
Nikki's passion for education led her to pursue additional qualifications, including a Certificate in English for Teaching Adults from Cambridge University in 2023.
She has since utilised her expertise to support Ukrainian refugees in accessing employment within Edinburgh through English language instruction.
Nikki is also qualified in teaching study skills at the college and university level, with a particular focus on supporting individuals with dyslexia and additional learning needs.

Online Learning: Embrace the Future of Education

At Centrepoint, we understand that education is evolving, and we're at the forefront of this digital transformation. Our online learning platform offers a host of benefits that enhance the learning experience and prepare students for success in today's interconnected world.

Flexibility and Convenience
Online learning offers unparalleled flexibility, allowing students to access educational resources and participate in lessons from anywhere with an internet connection. Say goodbye to commuting and rigid schedules. With online learning, you can choose the time and location that works best for you, whether it's early morning, late at night, or during your lunch break.
Personalised Attention
Our virtual classroom provides a personalised learning environment where students receive individualised attention from experienced tutors. With smaller class sizes and interactive features, students have more opportunities to engage with the material, ask questions, and receive tailored feedback.
Access to Resources
Our online platform is a treasure trove of educational resources, including interactive lessons, multimedia content, and digital textbooks. From maths and literacy exercises to educational videos and interactive quizzes, our platform offers a diverse range of resources to support every learner's needs.
Enhanced Collaboration
Online learning encourages collaboration and teamwork, allowing students to connect with peers from diverse backgrounds and exchange ideas in a virtual setting. Our virtual classroom features interactive tools that facilitate group discussions, collaborative projects, and peer-to-peer feedback, fostering a sense of community and belonging.
Technology Integration
In today's digital age, technological literacy is essential for success. Our online learning platform equips students with valuable digital skills that are increasingly in demand in the workforce. From navigating virtual classrooms to using productivity tools and multimedia software, students gain hands-on experience with technology that prepares them for future success.
Accessibility and Inclusivity
Online learning breaks down barriers to education by providing access to quality instruction for students with diverse learning needs and abilities. Whether you're a busy professional, a parent juggling multiple responsibilities, or someone with physical or geographical limitations, online learning offers a flexible and inclusive solution that accommodates your unique circumstances.
Real-World Preparation
Our online learning platform simulates real-world environments and interactions, preparing students for the digital challenges they'll encounter in their academic and professional lives. By honing critical thinking skills, problem-solving abilities, and digital literacy, students develop the confidence and competence they need to thrive in an increasingly interconnected world.
